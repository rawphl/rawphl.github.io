GLUMI is the world's first commercially available, non-invasive & affordable continuous glucose monitor. It is designed to accommodate everyone's different bodies' shapes & sizes, and it is optimized for maximum comfort. We promise that you'll barely even notice wearing your GLUMI - if at all.

Receive seriously accurate analytical data about your blood glucose levels, stress levels, your daily activity, recovery & sleep. Get up to 1'500 precise measurements per day. With the help of our complex algorithms we can process your data and give you personalized tips and recommendations for health, longevity & being happy living in your own body.

Due to its minimal size & comfortable materials, GLUMI attaches itself to your body in a seamless & pain-free way. The basic starting kit comes with three different types of attachment straps, so you can choose the one that suits you best.
