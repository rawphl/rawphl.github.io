    • FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

    ?

    May I use the sounds which I've purchased from you for commercial projects?

    If you have purchased one of our sound packs, you are free to use its sounds in a commercial way.

    ?

    Why are the downloaded file sizes so big?

    We want to provide you with the best possible sample sound quality which is why we sell lossless audio files. As a result, the files end up being quite large. Feel free to compress the files to your own liking.

    ?

    Do I need to credit you if I use your sounds?

    No, you don't have to provide credit. We would be appreciative of you doing so but it is not a requirement.

    ?

    How do I know if a sound pack contains a specific sound that I'm looking for?

    If you are only looking for a very specific sound and are unsure whether it exists in a sound pack or not, feel free to shoot us a message, so we may provide you with further details.

    ?

    Is it possible to purchase only a few select sounds from a sound pack?

    Usually, no. But feel free to contact us so that perhaps we can work something out.
